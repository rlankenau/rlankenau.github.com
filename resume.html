About Me

I've been a professional developer for the last ten years. I started out working with web technology, and then moved into the networking space. I was part of the team at 29West that built the product that eventually became Informatica Ultra Messaging when Informatica acquired 29West. I worked with big data analysis at MapR, and I've contributed to the Aeron high speed transport.

I spent some time working with a few friends, and we built Delve Deeper and Mega Ran in Language Arts. I've given various talks on building Indie Games at DePaul University, C2E2, and the Midwest Game Developers Summit.

I founded, and currently serve as the president of, Workshop 88, a hackerspace in Glen Ellyn, Illinois. I also helped found Spacelab, a hackerspace in Mokena, Illinois.

If you'd like to know more about my professional life, you're welcome to take a look at my resume, or connect with me on LinkedIn.

Most of my open source projects are hosted on GitHub. Some of them are detailed in the Projects section of this site.
