Makerspaces and Hackerspaces

Workshop 88 Electronics Lab

I'm the president of Workshop 88, a hackerspace in Glen Ellyn, Illinois. I founded the space about six years ago with a handful of friends, and we now have about 30 active members. Our goal in founding the space was to provide a place where we could go to work on projects, and a community of like-minded people who could provide a sounding board for our ideas.

As our membership has grown over the last six years, we've expanded beyond our initial goals, and we're doing a lot more outreach into the community. In addition to providing space for people to work on projects, we also work with local schools and libraries to extend their educational capabilites on topics that we find interesting. We teach one or two classes a week during the summers at area libaries, and do less frequent classes at middle schools and high schools during the school year.

We also participate each year in the Chicago Northside Mini Maker Faire and the Chicago Southland Mini Maker Faire, as well as the WCRA Mid-Winter Hamfest.

For more information (or to come join us!), email info@workshop88.com.
