Woodworking

I do various types of woodworking. These are some examples of woodturning projects from 2017.

An 8" spalted maple bowl

Spalted maple from a tree in my parents' yard in Naperville, finished with gloss spray lacquer. I love the way the bowl opens up, and the lacquer really makes the spalting pop.

A top made from cherry and maple

Hard maple and cherry, finished with paste wax. The spin is a bit eccentric, probably due to the very rounded tip.

A low 11" spalted maple bowl (in progress)

This is about the largest bowl my lathe (a Craftsman #113238180 monotube) can handle.
